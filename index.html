About O.B. Montessori

O.B. Montessori Center, Inc. is a private educational institution that offers a comprehensive Montessori education program for students from preschool to high school. Founded in 1987, the school is committed to providing a nurturing and stimulating environment that fosters the holistic development of each child.

The school's curriculum is based on the Montessori method, which emphasizes hands-on learning, self-directed activity, and collaborative play. O.B. Montessori Center, Inc. aims to cultivate critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning in its students.
